BEYOND BOUNDARIES 邊界
以外

當身處房間內,透過窗戶的框架向外望去,可以找到無限的可能性。同樣的,當完成一件作品,視覺上往往被紙張
或畫面中的四個邊界所侷限。然而,透過「邊界以外」的理念傳達,鼓勵觀者打破這些限制,探索框架外的未知
領域。身處社會中,人們常被無形的規範束縛,但在設計時,卻可以超越框架的束縛,進行創新與構思。期許觀者
在未來的人生旅途上,持續望向邊界以外,探索更多可能。
When standing inside a room and looking out through the frame of a window, one can discover
infinite possibilities. Similarly, when completing a piece of work, the visual presentation is often
confined by the four edges of the paper or canvas. However, through the concept of "Beyond
Boundaries," viewers are encouraged to break free from these limitations and explore the un-
known realms beyond the frame. In society, people are often constrained by invisible norms, but
in design, it is possible to transcend these boundaries and innovate freely. It is my hope that view-
ers will continue to look beyond boundaries in their life journeys, seeking endless possibilities.
